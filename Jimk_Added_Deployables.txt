Ver 1.8 Updated for week 151, Removed Damage Modifiers.

Adds 4 new Deployable Items to the game, all crafted on the Fabricator bench.

Jimk_Xtra_Large_Aquarium - An Aquarium the size of 1 floor piece, and as high as a standard wall.

Water Extractor -  A piece of equipment compresses the ground to access the water below.

Vapor_Collector - Uses Electricity and a large aluminum cone to charge the air and remove all moisture from it.

Large WindTurbine - A Large WindTurbine that produces three times the electricity of the regular turbine at about double the cost.
