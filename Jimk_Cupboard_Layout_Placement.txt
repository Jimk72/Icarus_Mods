Ver. 1.0

Craftable on player, This will allow you to place either a side by side grid for 3 storage cupboards or front to back for 5 storage cupboards. These snap to each other so you can do up to 9 side by side or 15 front to back. They also snap to stack so you can have 2 rows of storage cupboards that are all perfectly lined up. Once you have placed all you cupboards, you do not need to merge the mod in as the cupboards will stay where they were placed. For this to work the rotation has beed removed from deployment of the cupboards but just remove the mod after they are placed and your back to normal for individualy placing cupboards.
