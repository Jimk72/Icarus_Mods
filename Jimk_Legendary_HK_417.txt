Legendary HK 417

Cost for basic weapon: 800 ren
Cost per Upgrade: 10 Exotics

Allows Custom versions to focus on Damage, Stealth, Distance, Quickness, or any combination in between.

Upgradable Sights, Barrels, Attachments, and Magazines.

40+hrs in the making to get the silencer and other upgrades to properly function, create all the different models that are interchangable and package them all up into a working Legendary Weapon that is fully customizable.
